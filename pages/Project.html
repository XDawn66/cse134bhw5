The Snake Game

Created a classic snake game with C++ and conio.h library. The snake can grow longer and longer as they eat more fruits on the map. The player will use WASD keys to control the snake and lose if their snake eats its tail or hit the edge of the map.

The previous version of the app had bugs is now fixed.
